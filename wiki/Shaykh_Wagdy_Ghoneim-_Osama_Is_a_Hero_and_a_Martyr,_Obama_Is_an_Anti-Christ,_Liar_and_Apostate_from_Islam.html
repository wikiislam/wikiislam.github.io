Translated from the original Arabic by Al Mutarjim

This is an excerpt from a 15-minute video posted on YouTube, May 2, 2011, which contains Egyptian Shaykh Wagdy Ghoneim's comments on the killing of bin Laden.

In the name of Allah Most Merciful.

This is my comment on the news, if it is true, of the martyrdom of the great ascetic hero, the eminent Shaykh Osama bin Laden.

In the name of Allah the Most Merciful, the Lord of the Worlds. Peace and blessings of Allah be upon the master of the first and the last, as well as upon his family and companions and whoever follows him in righteousness until the Day of Judgment. May Allah be pleased with them all.

That anti-Christ, the liar named Obama, the apostate from Islam, has attacked us. ...
