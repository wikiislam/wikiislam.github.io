If Islam was the right extension of the other religions, which are the distorted words of god, how come they are only mentioned in Islam? Islam is self-contradictory. It says that nobody is perfect like Allah, then all the sheikh's refer to Muhammad as a perfect man. By using the simplest rule of logic it will lead to the conclusion that Muhammad is Allah. Of course not because he died in front of a lot of people.

I hate every single myth that is made up by Muslims to prove that they are right, even if those myths defies all the known rules of science and nature. and if you argue or open your mouth you will be discriminated against. Muhammad loved sex. He knew that men loved sex, so he made sex available for every man - while women are just another property item of a man. Period. If Islam is a religion of tolerance, how come all the people who convert are treated with extreme hostility? You always hear stories of parents who died because their sons converted. These stories are told just to make you feel guilty, and even afraid to show any sign of objection on any rule that's set by Islam.

Also in the books that talk about the day of judgment they say that sun will rise from the west, anyone who studied basic physics knows that for the sun to rise from the west, the earth must change its direction of rotation. Changing the direction of rotation at such speed will tear the earth's crust apart. also the earth is stable because of its core not because of stupid mountains. The mountains don't have souls, they are merely piles of dust or rocks that resulted from the friction between the layers of the earth. If a mountain see's god there is no way it's gonna bow for him or become destroyed.

Moses killed a pharaoh then god just forgave him. There is no way Noah's Ark could have tolerated the weight of all the animals. What if one of the animals he had got fat?
