The most complete list of websites related to Islam

Featured Site
Money Jihad
"This blog is dedicated to exposing how the enemies of America finance themselves. Here we shine a light on: Terrorist funding, The economies & military expenses of Middle Eastern countries, Sharia finance, Islamic taxation"

Arabic

Annaqed
Site in Arabic and English. "Annaqed’s main objective is to stand up for human rights all over the world but particularly in the Middle East, opposing all forms of oppression and exposing the roots of terrorism... Islam remains Annaqed’s primary focus, for we strongly believe that this ideology is the main source of terrorism"
Arabic Bible
Web site by Christians for Muslims in English and Arabic. "Our sincere desire is for all Arabic speaking people on the Internet to be introduced to God's eternal plan for men's salvation through Jesus Christ the Messiah"
Father Zakaria
Website of Coptic priest, Zakaria Botros, often referred to as "Islam's Public enemy No. 1". In August 2010, a fatwa was issued against him, and Al Qaeda has put a bounty of 60 million dollars on his head. In comparison, the reward placed on Osama bin Laden's head by the U.S. government was 25 million dollars
Investigate Islam
Site by Christian Prince in English and Arabic. "While your browsing around the site, don't forget to check out our videos. We have Hundreds of our video's posted for free to teach you about Islam"
Muslims for Christ
Website by converts to Christianity, in English and Arabic. Featuring news, testimonies, and a forum

Bengali

Mukto-mona
Site in Bengali and English. Focuses on science and the Qur'an. "Mukto-Mona is one of the most frequently visited Bengali and South Asian secular humanist websites, chiefly run by a team of dedicated volunteers"
Taslima Nasrin
Former Muslim from Bangladesh, whose books have been banned in her country. In 1993, Islamic fundamentalists announced a Fatwa against her, offering a bounty for her death

Czech

Fight Islam
"The aim of the website is to inform you about what is by far the largest problem in the present time, an issue which people today do not speak much of probably because of ignorance. The name of that problem is Islam"
Pravda o Islamu
Czech site, mirror of the site by former Muslim and author of "Understanding Muhammad", Ali Sina, with articles, debates, and news updates

Danish

Islaminfo
"Read the facts about religion 'Islam', Read the Quran - Islam's holy scripture, Search for relevant material in our article database, Search our large Encyclopaedia of Islam, Download leaflets about Islam that you can print and distribute"

Dutch

AANNEEMBAARHEIDSGODSDIENST
Atheist blog about Islam and Abrahamic religions from Netherlands
Het Vrije Volk
Dutch secular right-wing website Het Vrije Volk (The Free Citizenry). Covers a variety of subjects including: Islam and islamification, multiculturalism and corruption in the government
Zentralrat der Ex-Muslime
Dutch website for atheist apostates of Islam

European

Gates of Vienna
"At the siege of Vienna in 1683 Islam seemed poised to overrun Christian Europe. We are in a new phase of a very old war." Active blog with daily articles about the Islamic threat for Christian Europe

French

AthéTürk
Forum for atheist Turks living in France. website in French
À vrai dire
French site of Antisharia.com. "Fighting Radical Islam-A site of information, confrontation, debate, exposing the true Face of Islam"
Bivouac-ID
A French-language website also with an active forum. Has "published over a thousand articles translated from the foreign press, numerous pictures and cartoons, and subtitled tens of videos"
Islam-documents.org
According to the website, 2700 pages of original documents relating to the development of Islam. "This set of over 10,000 texts (about 1700 pages of paper) is designed for Western audiences, who have no access to the sources"
Islam La
French site for apostates of Islam. "Forum des ex-musulmans orthodoxes"
L'islam, religion ou systeme politique?
"Islam, Religion or Political System? (Important note: Sufism, Ahmadiyah, etc ... have nothing to do with the study that follows. This is rather an attempt to understand why Islam violent world"

German

Bürgerbewegung Pax Europa
Bürgerbewegung Pax Europa (BPE) is a German group which describes itself as a "Human Rights Organization for Freedom and Democracy Against Islamization"
Bürgerforum Hannover
One-page website, providing a "political analysis of the Islamic terror, [with] Quotes from the Koran, the Al-Qaida training manual and other sources"
Bürgerinitiative Wertheim
"The subject of Islam, Muslims and mosques in Germany" German citizens protest against building a mosque in their town
Minority Report
"On this page you can download an essay about the creeping submission of Europe through islam. The PDF-document contains 496 pages and more than 200 graphics and pictures"
Weltherrschaft
Religion and Politics in Islam. "Will Islam rule the world? Religion, political system and social order"

Italian

Italian FFI
An Italian version of the site by former Muslim and author of "Understanding Muhammad", Ali Sina, with articles, debates, news updates, and an active Forum
UnpoliticallyCorrect
Italian blog about Islam.

Malay

Indonesian FFI
An Indonesian version of the site by former Muslim and author of "Understanding Muhammad", Ali Sina, with articles, debates, news updates, and an active Forum
Faithfreedompedia
The official Wiki for the Indonesian chapter of Faith Freedom International

Norwegian

FOMI
Site of Forum against Islamization This Scandinavian site is mainly in Norwegian, with some information in English

Persian (Farsi)

Derafsh-Kaviyani
Website by Zoroastrians, in English and Persian, about Islam in Iran. "Our goal with this site is to open up a space to discuss and to exchange ideas about issues although rational but still forbidden to talk about. Issues about Islam, history of Islam, Iranian history, Islam in Iran, analysis of parts of Quran..."

Polish

Arabia chrześcijańska
Christian Arabia is about Arabic speaking Christians living in Islamic countries, like the Assyrians, Chaldeans, Maronites, Copts, Armenians, Greeks, Christians in Turkey, Christians in Palestine, Maghreb, the Arabic Peninsula and the Middle East. It includes testimonials from ex-Muslims and some polemical articles about Islam
Religia Pokoju
Anti-Islamic website in Polish. Includes am article on FGM (Female Genital Mutilation)
Europa21
Polish portal on Islam

Portuguese

Observatório da Jihad
Portuguese blog about Islam

Romanian

Realitatea lui Petria
Blog in Romanian

Russian

Islam Uncovered
A Russian-language website. "Are Islamic terrorists Muslims or can they simply not be called such a thing? What is the status of women in Islam and Christianity? What is Sharia law in theory and in practice? Does Islam permit Muslims to have sex slaves? Does Islam allows pedophilia through marriage with children?"

Spanish

Informe sobre el Islamismo
One of the first websites in Spanish critical of Islam
Martinito
Blog by Spanish anti-Islamist
Mundo Sin Islam
Spanish site run by Imran Firasat, "an ex-Muslim from Pakistan, residing in Spain with political refugee status". "Fighting against the radicalism and terrorism of Islam"

Swedish

Hodjas Blog
Swedish blog on Islam and related current affairs
Islam i Sverige: Jihad mot folkhemmet? (Islam in Sweden: Jihad Against the Swedish Welfare State?)
This site is about a campaign against Islam by the Swedish nationalist party Nationaldemokraterna. They've made a brochure written in Swedish that one can read online
Islamkritik
Swedish blog critical of Islam, featuring related current affairs
Tidens Tecken
This site has several articles about Islam and information on persecution against Christians in Islamic countries

Multiple languages

Answering Islam
One of the most accurate, thoroughly referenced, and comprehensive sites on the net. Available languages include; Albanian, Amharic, Arabic, Bengali, Chinese, Dutch, English, Finnish, French, German, Hindi, Indonesian, Malay, Persian, Portuguese, Russian, Swedish, Tamil, Telugu, Thai, Turkish, and Urdu
Arab World Ministries
Site in Dutch, English, and French. "The Arab world is one of the least reached regions of the world. We want to see that change as God provides opportunities for us to share our faith, disciple believers, train leaders and be an encouragement"
Ayan Hirsi Ali
Website of Dutch politician Ayan Hirsi Alì, in English, French, German and Dutch. Ayan is an atheist libertarian ex-Muslim, from Somalia
Manifeste de Libertés
Manifest of Freedom by people from an Islamic background, Muslims as well as ex-Muslims. Manifest available in French, English, Arabic and Italian
