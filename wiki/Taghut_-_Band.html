Taghut (Band)

From WikiIslam, the online resource on Islam
Jump to: navigation, search

Taghut is an anti-religious black metal band most notable for their explicitly anti-Islamic music. Founded in 1999 as 666. They changed their name to Branded 666 in 2001, before finally settling with Taghut in 2007.

The band's name, Taghut, was taken after their September 11, 2008,[1] debut album, "Ejaculate Upon the Holy Qur'an", was recorded. It is an Islamic term referring to various evils in an Islamic sense, such as worshiping deities other than Allah, worshiping associates alongside Allah or ruling besides the laws and evidences (Qur'an, Sunnah, Usul-ul-fiqh) of Islam.
