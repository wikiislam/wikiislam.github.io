Open Season (Stuck Mojo)

Jump to: navigation, search

Open Season is the sixth track from "Southern Born Killers", the fifth studio album from Stuck Mojo, an American rap metal band. Stuck Mojo is considered to be one of the pioneers of rap metal.[1][2] They have toured alongside bands such as Machine Head and Slapshot, and their third album, Rising, sold over 3 million copies and remained Century Media's highest selling album until 2004, when Lacuna Coil's fifth album Comalies broke that record. The band disbanded in 2000, reforming five years later. In 2006, their original vocalist, Bonz, was replaced by the African-American rapper Lord Nelson.
