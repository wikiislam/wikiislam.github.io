This is a disclaimer for essays/op-eds that are hosted on WikiIslam. Please note:

  • Like our ex-Muslim testimonies, views expressed in an essay/op-ed belong to the author and are not necessarily endorsed by WikiIslam. Thus, they may contain original research/theories at odds with the rest of our content.
  • Because essays/op-eds, by their very nature, contain some amount of personal opinion, they may not necessarily comply with the guidelines which apply to most of the other content on the site (i.e. our encyclopedic and counter-apologetic content).
  • Although our standard policies and guidelines are not used, they must still comply with our less restrictive policies and guidelines for essays/op-eds (currently under construction)
  • The validity and accuracy of the views contained may not have been verified and do not have any connection with the rest of the content on the site.
  • If needed, essays/op-eds may be edited by us for relevancy, clarity and language issues although such changes are minor.

If you would like to submit an essay/op-ed, fill out this form.
