Bürgerforum Schlüchtern
"Ahmadiyya mosque in Schlüchtern. Blessing or danger...?" A site against the construction of further mosques in German towns
Christliche Mitte
Website covering the subject of the Islamization of European/Western societies

This site offers a number of essays on the subject of the Islamization of Europe
Disaffected Muslim
Blog by a disappointed convert to Islam. "The natterings of an unhappy American Muslim"
Efsha
"Efsha" means exposing in English. It is a site by a group of Rationalist Atheist Iranian ex-Muslims and has some detailed information. It has original content in Farsi and provides links to English articles from other websites
Egypt Weblog
Blog by Mamduh Schauki, an atheist from Egypt. The site covers "Egypt, Religion, Islam, Christianity, Secularism, Atheism, Egyptianism, freedom of religion, freedom of non-religion, etc..."
European Women
A group called European Women for Freedom discuss Islam and democracy, with special regards to the situation of women. The site was created on March 8, 2005, International Women's Day
France-Echos
"The little seed has sown France-Echos and other sites has finally sprouted. Now, in France, Europe and throughout the West, people are overwhelmingly aware of the danger posed by Islam. In France, thousands of sites and blogs have taken over, and even the media-political sphere, the censors can not hide reality"
Freedom Bulwark Resistance
A social networking platform, launched by Ali Sina, where individuals who are concerned with the spread of Islam "can meet, exchange the news, share ideas and put in action our plans". Also has its own forum
Gamla
"The non - profit organization "Gamla shall not fall again", was founded in December 1994... Since its foundation and today "Gamla Shall Not Fall Again" is in the forefront of the battle for the land of Israel, organizes activities, participates in demonstrations, and publishes articles, posters and stickers for that cause"
Glistrup Homepage
Website by the former Member of Parliament Mogens Glistrup. Contains downloadable books and articles about Islam and Islamic Immigration
Hindu Unity
"Islam's agenda for India is total conquer and conversion of it's citizens to Islam. If Hindus don't wake up, then we are doomed"
Infidels United
An "awesome social networking site very similar to facebook (except no facebook censorship)... Rules are very simple, you must be 13 years of age or older, don't encourage violence or post porn or harass other members or spam the site"
Isa al-Masih Tandoor
"'Isa al-Mesih ibn Miriam, Jesus Christ, (may his peace be upon us) was called the Prince of Peace and The Bread of Life... 'Isa al-Mesih claimed to be the bread that comes from God. This website is designed to clarify and understand his message"
Islam: The Greatest Threat
Archived version of the site. Discusses various Islam related topics, including; slavery, child marriage, contradictions, and more
Jihad Spin
"This site is about Jihad and Islam, not Muslims. It is to demonstrate that the fundamental teachings of Islam are incompatible with democracy, and the American way of life"
Lebanese Christian Congress
Mission Statement: "“Empower the Christians through knowledge and engagement to preserve the Lebanese Nation” We endeavor to secure a free and effective presence of the Christian community in Lebanon."
Martha Colmenares
"Un sitio sobre el acontecer nacional e internacional donde los hechos y sus huellas nos conmueven o cautivan" A very active blog from a woman in Venezuela
Pakistani Atheists & Agnostics
A "social organization for atheists, agnostics and freethinkers of Pakistan. We are affiliate members of Atheist Alliance International. As an organisation we promote secular causes and the separation of religion and state. We advocate the rights of women and minorities, as well as secular humanist values and free speech"
Pakistan: Terrorism
Articles on Pakistani sponsored Islamic terrorism. Informative links on the Islamic republic of Pakistan, and its role in international Islamic terrorism
Saffron Tigers
A Shiv Sena perspective. "Islam is the most violent and intolerant faith that has ever been presented to mankind... Islamic ideology is based upon an intense hatred of the non-Muslim. It has divided humankind into two perpetually hostile groups... the Muslims and the non-Muslims"
Sanningen om Islam
"This is an evangelical Christian site for the purpose of reaching out to Sweden's Christians with information about Islam's true nature. I am also addressing Sweden's Muslims with the good news of salvation"
Sekulyarizm.org
"The first site in Azerbaijan about science, philosophy and criticism. The site is about religions (mostly Islam) and supernatural powers. Religion is also criticized through scientific-philosophical ways. Our purpose is to show the lies of Islam and religions, and to get the people together for a secular society"
Shimshon 9
"Why Shimshon? The Shimshon unit, so named after the biblical warrior Samson, was established in 1997 as a Gaza Strip based battalion. The battalion was formed following the violent riots that erupted due to the Western Wall digs... Why ’9′? Nine in hebrew is ‘Teisha’... the meaning of Teisha is “alive and well”"
Stop Shariah Now
An in-depth site covering the various aspects of Islamic law. A project of ACT! for America. "Shariah is Islamic law and represents the traditional or “pure” normative interpretation of the Qur’an and teachings of Mohammed... It has remained largely unchanged for over 1200 years and is practiced today"
Un:dhimmi
"With a British perspective, this site is part of a wider attempt to educate the civilised world out of its complacency regarding radical Islam and Jihad; and the clear and imminent danger these represent"
