My Testimony of Leaving Islam

I just did not understand why congregation in the mosque came out after Friday prayers and attacked Hindus in India. I know of other religions after having visited their respected houses of worship, that were joyous and reflective of their own lives. (ignorance and political agenda of the mullah ).(a Mullah is a political "preacher-man" )and keeping Muslims terrorized and brainwashed is his game. Muslims are humans as any other and given a chance would certainly think rationally and ride themselves from the bondage that restricts their minds-starting with the their child and woman. Yes I do believe that my ancestors were Hindus and they were submitted to the sword of Islam and made to convert by human mughal invaders from Arabia. Ruled by tyranny and a semblance of benevolence with the loot, for mind and heart CONTROL of their submitters. Each mughal commander who then conquered a certain part of India proclaimed himself a king or emperor of that part and having lots of wives and concubines erected a monument in the name of his favorite one. Yes I am talking about Shah Jehan, Auranzeb, Tipu Sultan, Humayun, Mohammed Tuglag, etc. Who were these tyrants? None other than the modern day Bin Laden's all financed and sustained by riches from Gulf States - But in gods name? That’s a bluff, which the world has caught up to. Hinduism and Buddhism are to two peaceful religion of any times , even predating Christianity and Islam which were essentially belligerent. So do you two think you ought to take a leaf from peaceful pages of Hinduism and Buddhism? So what did Jesus preach? No conquest -only spread his message- love thy neighbor as you love yourself, peace to all mankind.

What did Mohammed preach and do? It is appalling to go into details of his life and deeds.

Control of the minds and countries of the Islamic people hitherto needed despots and tyrants, and dictators which subjugated the subject to abject ignorance and poverty and spiraling down further them the abyss of a man made cultism. But enlightened writers and leaders are paving the way for rescue of a billion humans -albeit slowly but surely with the inevitable bumps in the way. And the two humans who will receive and share the Noblest of any peace prize ever, in the near future are President George Bush and Salman Rushdie.
