Tabi' al-Tabi'un

From WikiIslam, the online resource on Islam
Jump to: navigation, search

Tābi‘ al-Tābi‘un (تابع التابعين‎) is the generation after the Tabi'un. Tabi' al-Tabi'un is a Muslim who had seen at least one of the Tabi‘un, was rightly guided, and died in that state. According to Prophet Muhammad, they are among the best generation of Muslims on Earth, along with the Tabi‘un and the Sahabah.
